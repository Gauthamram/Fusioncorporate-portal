Why Work for Us?

We take pride in our people, our brands and our commitment to providing quality to our customers. We offer a variety of career paths from retail to corporate head office and a positive place to work. We reward hard work and encourage fun and enjoyment along the way.

Benefits

Fusion Retail Brands offer a broad range of benefits to ensure our team is highly engaged and enjoys working for us!

We are devoted to support our people and work closely to attain future career goals and promote from within.

Our retail Team Members also have an exciting career path to move into our Support Office in various departments such as Marketing, Buying, Human Resources, Design and Fashion, Finance and IT to name a few.

Work with our friendly, fast paced, talented specialists and you also can receive:

  • - Unlimited earning potential in stores with our NEW Incentive Program!
  • - Amazing career opportunities and development
  • - Ongoing training opportunities
  • - 50% discount off all brands!
  • - Fashion styling techniques and product knowledge
  • - Eligible for Novated Lease with nlc.com.au
