Values

Leadership – we have the courage to lead with vision, to lead by example & inspire our people to take action.

Performance – we set challenging & measurable targets & deliver them.

Customer Centric – we place customers & stores at the centre of everything we do.

Scale, Speed, Simplicity – we leverage our scale by doing it fast & keeping it simple.

Maximise Value – we deliver value to employees, customers & shareholders by treating every dollar as if it’s our own.

Continuous Improvement – we seek opportunities to improve, to innovate & to set new standards.
