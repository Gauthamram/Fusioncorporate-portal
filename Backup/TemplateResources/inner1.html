Our Company

Fusion Retail Brands pty ltd is a leading national footwear and apparel retailer and wholesaler, with an Australian heritage dating back over 140 years.

We operate more than 430 stores in Australia and New Zealand. The group's portfolio of leading brands include Colorado, Mathers, Williams and diana ferrari.

Fusion Retail Brand’s vision is to develop a team of motivated people successfully working together to give our customers a great experience.

This section of the website provides you with information about our history, our future, our directors and senior management.

Our people are the heart of Fusion Retail Brands. Around Australia and New Zealand our spirited employees are passionate about our customers and brands. Click here to find out more about working with Fusion Retail Brands.
